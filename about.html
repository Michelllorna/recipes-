TIPS & TRICKS

It is important to keep from spreading germs!
- Before you get out ingredients for a recipe, wash the work area with a clean cloth, soap and water.
- Wash your hands with soap, scrubbing for at least 20 seconds.
- When you cook, don't lick the spoon or your fingers.
